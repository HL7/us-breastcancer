CodeSystems defined as part of this Implementation Guide:

Code Systems

Name Definition
BreastCancerMCategoryCS Codes that designate concepts describing Metastases category according to AJCC breast cancer staging recommendations.
BreastCancerNCategoryCS Codes that designate concepts describing Node category according to AJCC breast cancer staging recommendations.
BreastCancerStageCS Codes that designate concepts describing breast cancer stage group, according to AJCC breast cancer staging recommendations.
BreastCancerTCategoryCS Codes that designate concepts describing stages for Tumor category according to AJCC breast cancer staging recommendations.
BreastCancerTypeCS Histologic types of breast cancer. Codes are drawn from ICD-O-3.
BreastSiteCS Topography of the breast. Codes are drawn from ICD-O-3.
BreastSpecimenCollectionMethodCS The surgical method used to obtain the tissue sample.
BreastSpecimenTypeCS The type of specimen obtained from the breast.
ConditionCategoryCS
DegreeOfLymphaticInvolvementCS Value set containing the degree of lymphatic involvement.
DiagnosticServiceCS The type of laboratory service used in producing a laboratory result
DirectionalityCS Anatomical location or specimen further detailing directionality. Codes are from NCI
ExceptionValueCS Reasons that a value associated with a test or other finding is missing. Extended set based on http://hl7.org/fhir/ValueSet/observation-valueabsentreason
HER2ISHMethodCS Value set containing In Situ Hybridization methods of determining HER2 status
HER2MethodCS Value set containing methods of determining HER2 status
HER2StatusCS HER2 status overall result
LateralityCS Anatomical location or specimen further detailing the side(s) of interest. Codes are from NCI
LeftRightLateralityCS Simplified set of laterality codes indicating detailing the side(s) of interest. Codes are from NCI
MammaprintRiskScoreInterpretationCS Intepretation of Mammaprint Recurrence Score
MorphologyBehaviorCS The morphologic behavior of the cancer. These are the suffix to the ICD-O-3 histologic type codes
NuclearGradeCS The nuclear grade describes how closely the nuclei of cancer cells look like the nuclei of normal breast cells. In general, the higher the nuclear grade, the more abnormal the nuclei are and the more aggressive the tumor cells tend to be. The nuclear grade is a part of overall tumor grade.
OncotypeDxDCISRiskScoreInterpretationCS Intepretation of OncotypeDX DCIS (Ductal Carcinoma In Situ) Recurrence Score
OncotypeDxInvasiveRiskScoreInterpretationCS Intepretation of OncotypeDX Recurrence Score for invasive cancers.
PortionTotalityCS Further detail of the anatomical location or specimen detailing whether it includes the entire entity or only a part or portion.
PositiveNegativeEquivocalCS Interpretation of a test result as positive, negative or equivocal.
RecurrenceRiskScoreInterpretationCS Interpretations of risk as high, intermediate, or low. Answer set taken from LOINC LL3198-0
StageTimingPrefixCS When staging was done, relative to treatment events.
StagingMethodCS The methodology or standard used to do the staging.
StainingIntensityCS Part of the Allred scoring, based on the intensity of that staining, on a scale of 0 (Negative) to 3 (Strong).
YesNoCS
YesNoUnknownCS
